Bonded Labor

Bonded labor is possibly one of the largest slavery organization in the world. But it is also one of the least known types of slavery. Somebody becomes a bonded slave when labor is demanded as a way to repay a loan or debt. Bonded labor has existed for hundreds of years. It spreads from everywhere, from Asia to America.Bonded labor exist because of the discrimination and poverty the world faces. And despite slavery being illegal, many governments refuse to enforce the law.

Forced Labor

Forced labor is when someone is forced to work against their will under some form of punishment. Forced labor affects men, women, and children around the world.Almost all slavery practices such as bonded labor, have some type of forced labor element implemented into it. Forced labor is illegal, in international law and in most national legislation's around the world.

Sex Trafficking

Sex trafficking is one of the more known types of slavery. It exist through America, and globally. Sex traffickers use violence, lies, threats, and debt to force adults and children to have commercial sex against there will. How people get into sex trafficking can vary. Some are sold into sex slavery by there parents. Others are lured and tricked into false promises of a good job. It can last from one month to two years. Sex slavery is one the most dominant type of modern slavery in America today.
