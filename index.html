    The Fair will include:

  • Tables explaining how different countries are affected by modern day slavery. These reports will include the biggest supporters of these types of contemporary slavery and will give suggestions about how you can make a difference.
  • Playing an excerpt of a documentary about sweatshop labor.
  • A recorded interview with a survivor of human trafficking and slavery.
  • A survey to let you know how many slaves helped make what you have today.
  • A table where you can pledge to make a difference and nominate colleagues and friends to do the same.
  • A taste test of fair trade and conventional coffee.
  • Time to interact with students who want to make an impact in an important issue.
  • Light refreshments.
