Sex trafficking

The majority of sex traffickers kidnap innocent people to perform sexual acts. It is an inhumane process that takes advantage of the ignorance of multiple men and women.

Statistics

  • According to "www.Humantraffickinged.com", "Each year about 17,500 individuals are brought into the United States and held against their will as victims of human trafficking."
  • In 2014, the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children estimated that 1 in 6 endangered runaways reported to them were likely sex trafficking victims.
  • Humantraffickinged.com stated that "80% of trafficked persons are women and children."
  • 75% of underage sex trafficking victims said they had been advertised or sold online. (Report on the Use of Technology to Recruit, Groom and Sell Domestic Minor Sex Trafficking Victims ,Thorn 2015)
  • The average age of entry into the sex trade in America is 12 to 14 years old. (The National Report on Domestic Minor Sex Trafficking: America Prostituted Children Shared Hope International May 2009)

How It Happens

Sex traffickers use violence, threats, lies, debt bondage, and other forms of persuasion to compel adults and children to engage in commercial sex acts against their will.Some people become romantically involved with someone who abducts or manipulates them into prostitution. Others are forced by their parents in third world countries to sell sex for profit. The duration of the human prostitution can range from a few months or years to indefinitely.Victims of sex trafficking can range from U.S. citizens, foreign nationals, women, men, and children.
